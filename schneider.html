Challenge

Enhance Schneider’s freight search platform by improving search flexibility and efficiency, allowing users to quickly refine their results.

Opportunity

Design an intuitive search system that enables multi-capacity selection, mileage limits, etc, ensuring faster, more customizable load searches for drivers and logistics teams.










background

What is Schneider FreightPower?

This group project was undertaken as a part of an Industry Sponsored Project during Fall 2024 and was executed in collaboration with Schneider National's team.

Schneider National, a logistics leader,operates one of North America’s largest freight networks, with over 9,000 trucks, 17,000+ daily users.

Schneider FreightPower® is a SaaS platform with CRM capabilities, streamlining load matching, booking, and tracking for enhanced efficiency and transparency

The Challenge

Truckers & Brokers lack search flexibility.

Schneider’s freight search platform needed a more efficient and flexible search experience for drivers by improving accessibility and customization. While it provided essential load-matching tools, it lacked intuitive filtering options, multi-capacity selection, and travel constraints, slowing down the search process.

"The freight search engine is a core driver of Schneider’s business, but its limited filtering options and unintuitive layout hinder users from quickly finding relevant loads, reducing both efficiency and engagement."

SOLUTION

Trucker Search Optimization: Addressing Search Limitations in Freight Tools

The Enhanced Freight Search System creates a more efficient and user-friendly experience. Drivers and freight managers can customize searches with multi-capacity selection, set travel distance limits, and mobile-friendly filters, ensuring quicker and more relevant load matching.

SOLUTION #1

Multi-capacity Load Selection


  • •Users can now select multiple capacity types (e.g., Power-Only, Refrigerated, etc.) in a single search.
  • •This allows freight managers and drivers to view a wider range of options and customize their search criteria based on various types of freight, leading to better matches.
  • •Previously, only one capacity type could be selected, limiting search flexibility.









SOLUTION #2

Mobile Design


  • •The platform now has a mobile app design.
  • •Independent drivers can now easily access search filters and results on phones.
  • •This improves usability on mobile devices.









SOLUTION #3

Miles to Be Traveled Feature


  • •Users can set a maximum travel distance for loads.
  • •This ensures relevant results based on desired distances to be travelled.
  • •There was no such feature before.









SOLUTION #4

Above-the-Fold Search Interface


  • •Search filters are now visible immediately.
  • •Users can start searching without scrolling.
  • •Previously, filters were hidden below the fold.

To gain a comprehensive understanding of the requirements and preferences of both users and stakeholders, I conducted interviews with two Schneider stakeholders and a group of drivers and freight dispatchers. The questions focused on...

What key changes would improve the search system’s usability while meeting Schneider’s goals?
What does an ideal user experience look like, and what operational constraints should we consider?
What feedback have users shared about the current system’s functionality?

RESEARCH

What we Concluded...

By thoroughly analysis research findings and brainstorming potential solutions, I identified four key insights to improve the freight search experience. These insights focus on enhancing search flexibility, optimizing usability on mobile, and making critical features more accessible to streamline decision-making for users.

Expanding Search Flexibility

Allowing users to select multiple capacity types in a single search helps them find more relevant loads without running multiple queries.

Enhancing Mobile Usability

A mobile-friendly interface ensures independent drivers can easily access and filter loads on the go, reducing friction in the search process.

Personalized Search Efficiency

Features like saved favorite searches and setting a max travel distance help users tailor results to their specific needs, making the process faster and more relevant.

Streamlining the Search Interface

Placing filters above the fold ensures key search options are immediately visible, reducing cognitive load and improving the overall experience.

User Personas

I created five user personas to represent the key stakeholders in the freight search process. These personas capture the diverse needs, goals, and frustrations of different users, ensuring the redesign effectively addresses real-world challenges and improves overall efficiency.

Igor

Age: 40


  • "Finding freight for my trucks is something I need to do every day"

Background

  • Igor is a small fleet owner and dispatcher for his own micro carrier. He has been in the industry for 10 years and has 10 trucks. While he will still drive sometimes, his goal is to get out of the truck and just manage his business.

Goals & Motivations

  • •Grow his trucking business by securing consistent, high-paying loads for his drivers.
  • •Streamline operations so he can transition from driving to full-time fleet management.
  • •Improve load-matching efficiency to reduce empty miles and increase profitability.
  • •Book the freight easily without adding unnecessary complexity to his workflow.
  • •Stay informed about trending loads to make strategic business decisions.

Frustrations

  • •Delays in finding and booking loads impact revenue and efficiency.
  • •Limited visibility into load details makes it harder to choose the best options.
  • •Limited flexibility in adjusting search criteria forces repeated searches.

Core Needs

  • •Easy-to-use freight search with better filters and load-matching.
  • •Seamless booking & management to reduce manual effort.
  • •Mobile-friendly, language-accessible UI for convenience.
